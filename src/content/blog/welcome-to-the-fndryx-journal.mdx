Welcome to the FNDRYx Journal — where we publish what we're thinking, building, and learning as we develop the capital-readiness exchange.

You'll find a mix here: long-form essays on the structural problems in early-stage capital, shorter field notes on founder signals and readiness, announcements as we ship new infrastructure and welcome new partners, and the occasional guest piece from operators and capital providers we work with.

Less marketing, more thinking out loud. We publish when there's something worth saying.
